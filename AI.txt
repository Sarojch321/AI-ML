Artificial intelligence (AI) is a field of computer science that focuses on creating machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. This includes learning, problem-solving, decision-making, and comprehension. AI systems can process data, make predictions, identify objects, and even generate natural language. 
Key aspects of AI:
Simulating Human Intelligence:
AI aims to replicate human-like abilities, such as learning from experience and adapting to new situations. 
Machine Learning:
A core subfield of AI where algorithms learn from data to improve performance on specific tasks. 
Deep Learning:
A type of machine learning that uses artificial neural networks with multiple layers to analyze complex data patterns. 
Applications:
AI is used in various fields, including healthcare, finance, transportation, and manufacturing.
